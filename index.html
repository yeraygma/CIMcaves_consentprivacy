Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your personal data, respecting your privacy, and complying with international data protection laws including the GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), LGPD (Brazil), PIPEDA (Canada), Privacy Act (Australia), and PIPL (China). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you take part in our survey and use our services.

2. Data We Collect

  • Organization you work for
  • Type of organization
  • Job position
  • Marine ecoregion and locality where you conduct your research
  • Email - only if you request to recieve the survey results or you exercice any data protection right -

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The data collected is used solely for research purposes to understand human impacts on the biological communities of sea caves. Your data will not be shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our processing of your personal data is based on your consent, which you provide by participating in our survey.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your data, including:

  • Encryption in Transit: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored on our servers is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your data and they are trained on data privacy principles.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for this research. Once the research is concluded, your data will be anonymized or securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction of Processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time, which will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • Withdrawal of Consent to Receive Results: In case you have requested the survey results you can withdraw at any time that consent. Your email address be removed from our mailing list and no longer you will receive the final result.
  • Modification of Email Address: If you need to update or correct your email address, either because of an error or a change of address, you can request the modification. Remember that will be necessary to include the survey unique identifier to locate your old email.

8. International Data Transfers

If we transfer your data outside of the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure it is protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Contact Us

If you wish to exercise any of your rights, please use a link to a form located at the end of this page or at the navigation bar Rights request form

10. Google Anayltics

We may use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, to collect information about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to gather data such as the IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the website. This information helps us understand website traffic and improve user experience.

Google may process this data on servers located in various countries, which could involve data transfers outside your home country. All data collected through Google Analytics is anonymized and cannot be linked to any personally identifiable information.

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Exercising Your Rights

Given that this survey does not collect personal identifiers such as names and emails are not mandatory, we have implemented an anonymous unique identifier system to enable you to exercise your data protection rights.

Upon completing the survey, you will receive a unique identifier. Please save this code. If you wish to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your responses, or exercise any other data protection rights, you can use this identifier to submit your request.

Email only will be requested either if you desire to recieve the survey results before been published or exercice any data protection right.
